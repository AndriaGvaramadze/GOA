The Christ the Redeemer statue (Cristo Redentor) is located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and is one of the most famous symbols in the world. Completed in 1931, it stands 30 meters tall, with an additional 8 meters for its base. The statue is situated atop Corcovado Mountain and offers panoramic views of the city. Christ the Redeemer is considered a cultural and religious symbol of Rio de Janeiro and Brazil. In 2007, it was recognized as one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
